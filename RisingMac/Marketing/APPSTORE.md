## Description
**Rise toward your dreams, one deposit at a time.**

RisingMac is a calm, focused savings companion that lives right in your Mac's menu bar. Set goals, track deposits, and watch your progress — without the noise of traditional finance apps.

**What makes it different:**
- **Menu bar simplicity** — Your goals are always one click away, never buried in an app you forget to open
- **Progress visualization** — Beautiful rings and milestones show exactly how far you've come
- **Smart insights** — AI-powered analysis helps you understand your savings pace and predict when you'll reach each goal
- **Flexible goals** — Create unlimited savings goals with custom targets, deadlines, and icons
- **Privacy-first** — All data stays on your Mac. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

**Perfect for:**
- Emergency funds
- Vacation savings
- Major purchases
- Investment goals
- Any financial dream you want to track

Download RisingMac and start moving toward your goals today.
